Ranks of demon

The ranks of demons in Demon Slayer are divided into two groups: the Twelve Kizuki and unranked demons. The Twelve Kizuki are the 12 strongest demons, split into six Lower Ranks and six Upper Ranks. The Upper Ranks are the strongest of the Twelve Kizuki, with Upper Rank 1 being the strongest overall, and Lower Ranks are less powerful than Upper Ranks. here is a video for reference

Ranks of demon slayer

The ranks of the Demon Slayer Corps are divided into ten tiers, from lowest to highest: Mizunoto, Mizunoe, Kanoto,Kanoe, Tsuchinoto, Tsuchinoe, Hinoto, Hinoe, Kinoto, and Kinoe. The top-tier rank is the Hashira, also called the Pillars, who are the most powerful swordsmen in the Corps and have their own separate system.
